NYCBA 1 On 1 League

Rules For Tournament

1) Game is 16 by 1's and 2's.

2) Player must win by 2 points or more.

3) 6 foul limit. If a player receives 6 fouls, they foul out of the game and forfeit the match regardless of score.

4) Take backs on rim hit only, take ball back to the three point line.

5) Who receives ball first? At the start of each game, Players will shoot from the 3 point line, first player to hit and other player not match receives the ball first.

6) Change of Possession - The ball is exchanged after every made basket. There will be no "make-it-take-it" rule.

7) Ball Out-of-Bounds - The ball will be taken out from the top of the key.

8) Sportsmanship - Good sportsmanship is expected from all participants and spectators. poor sportsmanship can lead to ejection by referee.

9) NYCBA Officals have final say on all decisions.

10) In the unfortunate case of a severe player injury, player that is injured and unable to continue playing will have to forfeit match.
